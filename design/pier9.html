Designing Pier 9 Vitual Expereience

Autodesk, the 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software company, built the Pier 9 space in a historic pier shed on San Francisco’s waterfront, situated to take advantage of the vibrant urban skyline on the bay. The design team created a virtual experience that allows visitors to explore the space, learn about the process of making things, and understand the history of the Pier 9 waterfront. The site is also designed to highlight the working industrial facilities and the state-of-the-art collection of production machines and tools at Pier 9. From highly engaging interactive elements to use of compelling imagery, the Pier 9 website invokes a “you are there” sense of place and community.

Role: Lead Design, Art Direction
