Designing Autodesk Fusion 360

Autodesk Fusion 360 is a cloud based 3D CAD software that allows users to design, engineer and fabricate products in one integrated tool. It is Autodesk’s newest effort to lead the future of making things in both manufacturing and consumer market. Unlike many other 3D CAD software, Fusion 360 is simple and intuitive for achieving the most complex forms and functions. The product also targets a new demographics with users who are youthful, creative and independent.
