2013

Voting, Simplified.

The team at TurboVote saw our complicated voting system and realized it could (and should) be easier. So, they created a simple registration and voting tool. After answering some basic questions, TurboVote fills out the paper work for you, sends you everything you need in the mail and then reminds you about upcoming elections. It allows you to vote and register wherever you are. It places all the necessary materials and all the necessary information, in your hands. By pairing technology and civic engagement, TurboVote connects citizens with their most essential democratic right. It's the simplest way to have a voice and be civically engaged. Simple.Honest.Work. was brought on to rethink the look and feel and create a cohesive, straightforward, trustworthy experience.
